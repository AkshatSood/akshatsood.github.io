About

Hi, my name is Akshat and I've been drumming since 2015. After a 2 year hiatus, I am trying to get back into drumming. For practice, I record drum covers on my kit (Alesis Crimson Mesh II Kit). This way I can share the music which I enjoy and learn new things along the way.

If you have any feedback, suggestions or recommendations, reach out to me on social media or directly through the contact form below.
